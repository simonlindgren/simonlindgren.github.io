    <p>I am Professor of Sociology at Umeå University in Sweden. My research concerns politics, power, and resistance at the intersection of digital technologies and society. I use critical discourse approaches, computational text analysis, and network methods to study movements, mobilisation, opinion, and identity.</p>
    <p>I also direct DIGSUM, an interdisciplinary research centre studying the social dimensions of digital technology, and am the editor-in-chief of the Journal of Digital Social Research (JDSR). My books include <em>Data Theory</em> (2020), <em>Digital Media &amp; Society</em> (2025, 3rd ed.), <em>Critical Theory of AI</em> (2024), and <em>Discourse Machines</em> (2026).</p>
